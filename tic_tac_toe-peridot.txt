1. General Information
    - Game name: Peridot
    - Number of players: 2
2. Equipment
    - Board dimensions: 3x3
    - List of pieces: A, V
    - Initial board setup: All spaces left blank
    - Piece distribution: Player 1 has infinite A’s, Player 2 has infinite V’s
3. Gameplay
    - Turn order: Each player takes one action per turn. Player 1 begins.
    - Valid player actions: Each player must place one of their pieces on an unoccupied space on the board.
    - Piece characteristics: Both piece types are equivalent, and may only be placed, not moved. Each may only be played by their respective player.
    - Win/loss conditions: The first player to fill an entire row, column, or 3-space diagonal with only their pieces wins. If neither manage it, it’s a tie.
4. Additional rules
    
    None
